1. Objective¶

A crime is any act or omission that violates a law which results in a punishment. Punishments can range from the payment of a fine to incarceration in jail. The level of the offense or crime will usually be set in proportion to the severity of the crime. A criminal definetly pays the price for its sins, but what about those innocent people who are victimized for unknowingly reasons. Why should they suffer?

"CrimeCure.inc" is started with a mission to keep people of the United States of America safe and to decrease the crime rate in the country. Crimes vary by jurisdiction—from state to state and town to town. For providing any solution, it becomes very much important to analyze the crimes in the repective jurisdiction. Therefore, We have decided to carry out the research that encover the insights of the Jurisdiction areas with Highest number of crimes reported per capita in the country for 2015 and present our new feature "Locate The Crime" to the executive officers of those jurisdictions.

For this, a dataset is provided to us that conatins the 40 years of data on the four major crimes the FBI classifies as violent — homicide, rape, robbery and assault — in 68 police jurisdictions with populations of 250,000 or greater. A detailed analysis is needed to understand the scenerio of crimes happening in the particular jurisdiction.

7. Summary¶

Inorder to conduct the research, a dataset was selected that conatins 40 years of data on the four major crimes the FBI classifies as violent — homicide, rape, robbery and assault. The dataset was further explored to know that, there are 2788 number of rows and 15 columns.

Moreover,to find the most unsafe scenerios for women in last 40 years sort by values was performed with variable "rapes_percapita". Then 5 series were drawn from the dataset with the highest number of violent crimes commited per capita for each crime seperately.

Finally a dataset for top5 jurisdiction with highest number of reported violent crimes is prepared. Multiple horizonatal bars were plotted in one chart to understand the relations between the types of crimes in the 5 most dangerous Jurisdiction areas of the country for year 2015 as per the available data. It turns out that St. Louis, MO', 'Detroit, MI', 'Memphis, TN', 'Milwaukee, WI', 'Baltimore, MD' are shortlisted for the most unsafe places in the united states. Out of which St. Louis, MO has the highest murder rate in America. Memphis, TN recorded the highest number of assaults per capita in the United States.
